About Us

Our story, our vision, and the family God is building in Thika

Our Story

Born from a Hunger
for God's Presence

Believers' Sanctuary Thika began with a small group of believers who refused to settle for religion without the power of the Holy Spirit. What started as a prayer gathering in a humble home in Gatitu grew into what it is today - a dynamic, life-giving spiritual community serving Thika and beyond.

Under the visionary leadership of Pastor Simon Njau, the church has grown from a handful of believers into a multi-ministry congregation, touching hundreds of lives across Thika County. Our growth has never been accidental - it is the fruit of unceasing prayer, biblical faithfulness, and an unwavering commitment to the Holy Spirit's leading.

Today, Believers' Sanctuary Thika stands as a lighthouse in Gatitu - a center of transformation, healing, discipleship, and Kingdom advancement. We are not merely a local church; we are an apostolic sending station, raising ambassadors of the Kingdom of God.

Our Foundation

Mission, Vision & Values

Our Mission

To make fully devoted disciples of Jesus Christ who transform their communities through the power of the Holy Spirit, grounded in the Word of God and fuelled by authentic worship.

Our Vision

To be a thriving, Spirit-filled apostolic center in Thika that raises Kingdom citizens who establish God's dominion in every sphere of society - family, workplace, government, arts, and beyond.

Our Mandate

Proclaiming freedom to the captives, healing to the broken-hearted, and the good news of the Kingdom of God to every person in Thika County and across the nations of the earth.

What We Believe & Value

01

The Authority of Scripture

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God - our supreme authority in all matters of faith and practice.

02

The Power of the Holy Spirit

We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, including the gifts of the Spirit operating in the Church today.

03

Authentic Worship

Worship is central to our identity - we pursue God's presence with all our hearts, in spirit and in truth, every time we gather.

04

Discipleship & Growth

We are committed to raising mature disciples who don't just attend church but live as the Church in every area of life.

05

Community & Family

We cultivate a culture of love, acceptance, and genuine belonging - no one walks this journey alone.

06

Kingdom Advancement

We exist beyond our four walls - our mandate extends to the streets, the marketplace, and the nations of the earth.

Lead Pastor

Pastor Simon Njau

Pastor Simon Njau is a passionate servant of God whose ministry has been marked by a deep hunger for the Holy Spirit, an unwavering commitment to biblical truth, and a burning love for the people of Thika.

Called into ministry over 15 years ago, Pastor Simon has faithfully shepherded the congregation of Believers' Sanctuary Thika with integrity, humility, and apostolic vision. His teaching ministry, both in-person and through digital platforms, has touched thousands across Kenya and internationally.

He is known for his ability to communicate deep biblical truths in clear, practical, and life-transforming ways. Under his leadership, the church has grown into a multi-ministry congregation with a strong online presence reaching believers across Africa and beyond.

"Freedom in Christ is not just a doctrine - it is the daily reality of every believer. The Arena of Liberty is a place where that freedom is not just preached but lived."

Pastor Simon Njau
